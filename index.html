Sustainable Harvesting

Every fall we harvest soapberries in the eco-reserves of Taiwan. Everything is protected and not a single tree cut down. Even better than organic, our soapberries are wild, sustainable and inspired by ancient traditions to preserve harmony and support a better world.

Zen Processing

After harvest, we dry the wild soapberries out in the warm southern sun, and when the conditions are perfect, we separate the seeds from the fruits. Nothing is wasted in processing, and especially not the soapberry seed, which we use to make sacred meditation beads.

Gentle Extraction

After separating the seed from each berry by hand, the fruits go through our patented sustainable extraction process. Inspired by ancient traditions, we gently extract the saponin-rich essence, blend it with other precious botanicals, and then transport to San Francisco.
